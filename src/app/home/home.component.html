Pricing Scenario Builder

Scenario Creation capability to plan for Pricing with the impact of price increases on volume and Mars/Customer P&L metrics. Data-Driven Price Simulation enables buy-in from key stakeholders in order to seamlessly execute pricing decisions.

Promo Tool

Promo tool which enables the stakeholders to optimize the promotion plans subject to various constraints & simulate various promo scenarios and understand the impact on financial metrics for MARS and the customers.

Price Execution Tracker

Executive Dashboard with the key metrics to track the pricing trends between Mars and main competitors. Objective is to have a one-stop shop for timely and precise price status data.

Price Pack Dashboard

The Pricing Analysis Platform is a tool that helps to understand the price trends across manufacturers, brands, pack types, etc across time. It allows the pricing team to identify the instances in which the products are under-priced or over-priced relative to the competitive positioning, where price adjustments are required, and thus helps in understanding and improving the overall effectiveness of the pricing strategy.
